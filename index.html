Who are we ?

We are five first-year students in HEC Paris, and together, as members of Esp'r, the association that promote sustainable development, we decided to launch a local grocery shop on the campus to reduce waste and promote organic and bulk goods. Even though for now we're still working on the idea before finally launching it for real, I've decided to create a website for our beautiful project.
